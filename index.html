About HotShotLashes

Our Story

HotShotLashes was founded in 2018 by professional makeup artist Jessica Monroe. After years of seeing clients struggle with fake eyelash application and care, Jessica decided to create a resource that would make lash mastery accessible to everyone.

What started as a small blog has grown into a thriving community of lash enthusiasts. Our mission is to empower people to enhance their natural beauty with confidence and skill.
